About Me

UX/UI Designer with roots in automotive & enterprise.

I'm a UX/UI Designer with a background in automotive and enterprise digital product design. My focus is on creating intuitive, user-centered experiences that balance functionality with strong visual craft. My work spans designing internal tools, connected vehicle interfaces, and consumer-facing products.

At Ford Motor Company and Harman International, I collaborated closely with engineers, product teams, and stakeholders to bring complex systems to life through thoughtful design.

I specialize in translating user needs and technical constraints into clear, engaging solutions through research, wireframing, prototyping, and design systems. I'm particularly passionate about accessibility, consistency at scale, and leveraging emerging tools like AI to improve both the design process and the end experience.

With a foundation in both UX and visual design, I enjoy working across the full design life cycle — from discovery and user research to high-fidelity execution and delivery. I'm driven by continuous learning, strong collaboration, and the opportunity to create meaningful, well-crafted digital experiences that make technology easier and more enjoyable to use.
